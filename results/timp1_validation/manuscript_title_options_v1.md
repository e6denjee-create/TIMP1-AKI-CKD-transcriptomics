   injury-repair and extracellular matrix remodeling in human kidney disease**

8. **Platform-specific integration of human kidney cohorts reveals a
   reproducible TIMP1-associated disease-state program**

9. **Transcriptomic context of TIMP1 across acute kidney injury, chronic kidney

10. **Cross-cohort and tubular-compartment validation of TIMP1-associated

## Recommended title

Option 1 is the clearest general-purpose title. It states the design,
association-oriented conclusion, principal biology, and human kidney context
without implying prediction, diagnostic performance, or causality.
