# Reviewer-driven robustness analyses (v5)

- The nine signatures were investigator-curated and frozen before GSE180394 analysis; they are not exact exports from a single database.
- Signature overlap and inter-signature correlations confirm that the scores represent related, non-independent injury-response programs.
- Broad-injury PC1 adjustment is a deliberately stringent sensitivity analysis. Attenuation is interpreted as evidence of shared injury-state variance, not as evidence against the unadjusted association.
- The external stringent-module correlation was 0.852. Against 1000 mean/variability-matched random modules, the empirical one-sided P value was 0.000999.
- Leave-one-discovery-cohort-out reconstruction tests whether external module replication depends on any single discovery cohort.

Limitations: these analyses do not remove unmeasured clinical severity,
cell-composition, treatment, or technical confounding. They strengthen an
association claim but do not establish TIMP1 specificity or causality.
