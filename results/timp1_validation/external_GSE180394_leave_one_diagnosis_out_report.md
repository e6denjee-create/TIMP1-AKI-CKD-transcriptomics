# GSE180394 Leave-One-Diagnosis-Out Report

Five diagnosis categories were evaluated: Diabetic nephropathy, FSGS/FGGS, IgA nephropathy, Lupus nephritis, Other kidney disease .

All 22 evaluated features retained positive median correlations across the leave-one-category-out iterations.
22 of 22 features remained positive in every iteration.
20 of 22 features remained FDR-significant in every iteration.

The analysis reduces concern that one major diagnosis category alone
accounts for the TIMP1-associated signature, module, or core-gene
pattern. It does not remove residual clinical heterogeneity or establish
direct regulation.
